Widgets

Widgets are modular content blocks you can place in the theme’s widget areas.

Widget Areas

AreaWhere it appears
SidebarRight column on posts, pages, and archives
Footer 1–3Three columns in the site footer
Before ContentFull-width area above the post body

Adding a Widget

  1. Go to Design → Widgets
  2. Select the widget area tab (e.g. Sidebar)
  3. Click Add Widget and choose a type
  4. Configure the widget’s title and options
  5. Click Save

Reordering Widgets

Drag the widget cards by their handle to change their order within a widget area.
