What is Pubvana?

Pubvana is a modern, lightweight CMS built on CodeIgniter 4 and Shield. It gives you a fast, secure blog and content platform with an intuitive admin panel — and a Premium Core add-on that unlocks analytics, scheduling, backups, and more.

Core Features

Posts & Pages

Rich content editing with drafts, revisions, scheduling, and bulk actions.

Themes & Widgets

Switch themes instantly. Drag-and-drop widget areas for sidebar and footer.

User Management

Five roles from Subscriber to Superadmin. Author profiles with bio cards.

SEO Tools

Per-post meta, XML sitemap, Google Analytics, social share on publish.

Marketplace

Install free and premium themes and widgets directly from the admin.

WordPress Import

Migrate posts, pages, categories, tags, and comments from WordPress WXR files.

⭐

Premium Core Premium

Analytics, post scheduling, backups, broken-link checker, affiliates, 2FA, and more.
