Premium Core Premium

⭐

This feature requires a Premium Core licence. Activate →

Premium Core is an add-on licence that unlocks eight advanced features in your Pubvana installation.

What Premium Core Unlocks

  • Analytics — page view tracking, top posts, referrer breakdown, daily charts
  • Activity Log — full audit trail of admin actions
  • Post Schedule — calendar view and automatic timed publishing
  • Backup & Export — one-click database + uploads ZIP download
  • Broken Link Checker — scan and report broken external links
  • Affiliate Links — create tracked /go/{slug} redirect links
  • Two-Factor Auth — TOTP-based 2FA for admin accounts
  • Premium widgets — Author Bio, Social Links, Table of Contents, Ad Unit/HTML

Purchasing a Licence

Visit pubvana.net/store and purchase the Premium Core product. After checkout you’ll receive a licence key by email and in your account dashboard.

Activating Your Licence

  1. In your Pubvana admin, go to Settings → Premium
  2. Paste your licence key into the Licence Key field
  3. Click Save & Activate
  4. Pubvana validates the key against pubvana.net and ties it to your domain
  5. Premium features appear immediately in the admin sidebar

90-Day Re-Validation

Pubvana automatically re-validates your licence every 90 days in the background. You do not need to do anything — as long as your licence remains active and your domain matches, the features stay enabled.

Licence Status Indicators

StatusMeaning
✓ ActiveLicence is valid and features are enabled
⚠ Expiring soonLicence renewal due within 14 days
✗ InvalidKey not recognised or domain mismatch — check your licence

Manual Re-Validation

php spark marketplace:revalidate

Run this command if you’ve renewed your licence or changed your domain and want to refresh the status immediately.
