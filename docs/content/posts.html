Posts

Posts are the primary content type in Pubvana — date-stamped articles that appear in your blog feed.

Creating a Post

Go to Posts → New Post in the admin sidebar.

  • Title — the post headline; slug is auto-generated
  • Content — rich-text editor (bold, links, images, lists, headings)
  • Excerpt — short summary shown in feed listings and SEO previews
  • Featured Image — shown at top of post and in social shares

Post Statuses

StatusBehaviour
DraftSaved but not visible to the public
PublishedLive and visible immediately
Scheduled PremiumPublishes automatically at the set date/time via cron

Categories & Tags

Select one or more categories from the list on the right. Add tags by typing them comma-separated in the Tags field — new tags are created on the fly.

Post Meta (SEO)

Expand the SEO panel below the editor to set a custom meta title and description for this post. If left blank, the post title and excerpt are used.

Social Share on Publish

If you have Twitter/X or Facebook configured in Settings → Social Sharing, a Share on publish checkbox appears. Tick it to auto-post when the article goes live.

Premium Post Flag Premium

Tick Premium content to paywall this post. Only users with a valid Premium Core licence and an active account can read it in full.

Bulk Actions

On the Posts list, tick the checkboxes and use the Bulk Actions dropdown to publish, unpublish, or delete multiple posts at once.

Post Preview

Click Preview (top-right of the editor) to open a new tab showing how the post will look on the live site before publishing.
