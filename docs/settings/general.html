General Settings

Go to Settings → General to configure site-wide options.

Site Identity

  • Site Name — appears in the browser title and header logo
  • Tagline — short description shown under the site name
  • Site Email — used as the From address for system emails

Reading

  • Posts Per Page — how many posts appear on the blog feed and category archives
  • Front Page — choose between the blog feed or a static page

Comments

  • Enable Comments — show or hide the comment form on posts
  • Comment Moderation — require approval or auto-approve new comments

Maintenance Mode

Toggle Maintenance Mode here to take your site offline temporarily. Admins and logged-in users can still access it. See Maintenance Mode for details.
